Unscramble Plugin Installation instructions:
	Drag all the .class files into your ImageJ program directoryâ€™s plugins folder (ImageJ > plugins)
	If ImageJ is already open on your computer, do one of the following two options:
		Restart ImageJ (preferred)
		Go to Help > Refresh Menus

How to use:
	Go to Plugins > Unscramble (should be at the bottom)
	Select the folder of images you wish to analyze
	Instructions then follow on the screen
		When inputting the pixel rearrangements, keep in mind that (0, 0) is the top left corner, with x increasing as you go to the right and y increasing as you go downwards

KNOWN ISSUES:
	Returns images as 32-bit, which make further analysis difficult
